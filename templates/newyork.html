Statue of Liberty

The Statue of Liberty, a colossal neoclassical sculpture on Liberty Island in New York Harbor, was a gift from the people of France to the United States. Dedicated on October 28, 1886, it has become an icon of freedom and of the United States.

  • Height: 151 feet (46 meters)
  • Dedicated: October 28, 1886
  • Annual Visitors: 4.5 million
